Overview

The FitFlow Health Dashboard is an all-in-one wellness application designed to centralize health monitoring and motivate users toward their physical goals. By combining real-time data tracking with a clean, calming interface, this project helps users stay focused, hydrated, and active every single day.

Features

  • Step Tracker: Real-time monitoring of daily steps, total distance (km), and calories burned.
  • Sleep Monitor: Analyzes total sleep time and quality against an 8-hour daily goal.
  • Exercise Suggestions: Curated workouts ranging from Quick Cardio and Strength Training to Yoga and "Study Break Moves."
  • Hydration Logger: Integrated water intake tracker to monitor daily glass counts.
  • Visual Progress: Interactive progress bars and goal-tracking percentages.

How It Works

  1. The main dashboard provides a quick snapshot of steps, calories, and hydration.
  2. The Step Tracker calculates physical activity and visualizes it through a "Weekly Progress" chart.
  3. The Sleep Monitor logs rest duration and provides a quality score to help users reach their targets.
  4. Users can select specific exercise categories to get immediate, focused workout recommendations.

Future Improvements

  • Live syncing with wearable heart rate monitors.
  • Push notifications for hydration reminders and movement alerts.
  • Social features to share weekly fitness achievements with friends.
